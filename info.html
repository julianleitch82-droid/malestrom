Diplomat Health Type

General nutrition and training-timing guidance from Jess, based on your health type. This is background info, not something you log — just good to keep in mind.

🍽️ Food

  • Most influenced by the time of meals
  • Longest gut — don't overload digestion with frequent meals
  • 2-3 good sized meals per day
  • Feel best skipping breakfast
  • Body responds better to more plant-based foods (eat more veg and salads)
  • Remove processed sugars — naturally susceptible to diabetes
  • Before 8am — liquids only
  • 8-11am breakfast — easy to digest (liquid-based if possible)
  • 2-4pm lunch — main meal
  • 6-8pm dinner — light meal

🏋️ Exercise

  • Do not cope well with stress (exercise) in the morning
  • Best to train in the afternoon / evening
  • Long, steady workouts — 1+ hours
  • 3x strength-based (weight lifting) workouts per week
  • 2x steady-state cardio sessions per week (rowing, hiking, swimming, boxing, walking)
  • Extended warm-ups needed — focus on mobility and activation

🔄 App Updates

If the app ever looks out of date after an update, tap this instead of digging through phone settings. It clears just this app's cache and reloads fresh — your logged workout history is kept.
